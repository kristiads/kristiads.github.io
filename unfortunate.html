A Series of Unfortunate Events

Adventures carry risk. So I had my share of 'accidents', especially in the first year that I rode my bike. I am grateful, though, that I have never been in a serious accident, and I attribute that to God's protection because I still consider myself a novice in motorbike riding. Though, my friend said that with every accident, one supposedly gets better at riding the bike.

So here's a list of a series of unfortunate events, and the lessons that I hopefully learn to avoid future mishap.

No Location Time Journey Description of Unfortunate Event Injury Lesson Learned
1 Rote Island, NTT 2012 On the way to church The first accident - motorbike slipped when going down on a rocky road Mild concussion Wear helmet ALWAYS!
2 Rote Island, NTT 2012 On the way to a friend's house in another village Ran over a confused puppy who was playing on the road; it died :( Death of a puppy Don't assume puppy knows where to run!
3 Rote Island, NTT 2012 On the way home from neighboring village Another motorbike, ridden by a teenager, crashed into mine while he was looking sideways and ran into my lane Wounds on right foot and toes Wear socks and/or shoes. You'd never know.
4 Rote Island, NTT 2012 On the way to the beach in the west coast Fast riding on a smooth paved road, when suddenly there was a pothole and I fell Scratches Listen to my mom - don't speed
5 Rote Island, NTT 2013 On the way home from a friend's house in faraway village at night A white dog ran across my path and I fell; the villagers said there was no white dog around there Mild scratches on the motorbike, but I was fine Wear a helmet that says "God sees". My students said that saved me.
6 Rote Island, NTT 2013 On the way from school to home While riding up a hill, the machine suddenly died and I fell My students who were riding with me had a trauma Pray daily; this could've happened the night before when I was on a more dangerous road
7 Rote Island, NTT 2013 On the way to a friend's house Took an unfamiliar shortcut on muddy road after the rain and got stuck in the mud; I had to push the motorbike Very dirty shoes Don't take unusual route, especially after the rain
8 Bromo, East Java 2017 Across the Bromo desert Slipped in the desert and the machine died because it was overheated after going up the mountain Landed on soft sand so no hurt! Rest the motorbike to avoid overheating
9 East Java 2017 Across (half of) the province Slipped on oil on the road while on high speed Scratches and mild concussion Listen to my mom - don't speed

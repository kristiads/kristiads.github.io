The Beginning

I did not know how to drive anything until I graduated from college. If it weren't because situation necessitated it, I would probably never learn how to ride a motorbike.

So, let's start from the beginning.

The Adventure

It turned out, I love riding a motorbike and feeling the wind on my face. I found riding a motorbike therapeutic, and I could drive for hours and hours. My record was 12-hour (almost) non-stop riding.

The Unfortunate Events

As there is an up, so there is a down. I have had my share of mishaps and near accidents. It is part and parcel of riding a motorbike, of life even. I have been blessed that despite my occassional recklessness, I have been safe so far.

Nonetheless, I took pride in my 'survival' and collect as honor badges series of unfortunate events.
